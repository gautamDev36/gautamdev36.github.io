Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

Welcome to WallePop. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. WallePop is designed to function as a standalone utility for your device.

2. Image Processing & AI Features

WallePop includes features like "AI Smart Crop" and "Blur Effects" to enhance your wallpaper experience.

  • Local Processing: All image analysis and processing (powered by Google ML Kit) happen entirely on your device.
  • No Uploads: Your photos are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. Permissions & Privacy

WallePop follows a "Zero-Permission" approach to storage:

  • Android Photo Picker: We use the official Android Photo Picker system component. This ensures the app only has access to the specific images you explicitly choose to add.
  • No Legacy Storage Access: Version 3.0.0 and above does not require broad "Read External Storage" permissions.

4. Advertisements

The free version of WallePop uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data according to its own privacy policy: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Updates are effective immediately upon being posted on this page.
